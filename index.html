About

Spanish Works provides translation and interpreting for business owners who want to communicate across cultures with accuracy. Whether you are a small business owner who wants to increase your clientelle base by marketing to the Hispanic community (35% in Providence) or a lawyer needing a certified translation to use in court, Spanish Works is here for you.

Founder of Spanish Works

Rebecca Rhoads

President of Providence Toastmasters, part of an international public speaking organization. Rebecca works to empower her clients to identify, track, and achieve their goals. Rebecca previously taught Spanish at the University of Rhode Island and is currently growing her business and leading workshops for the Center for Women and Enterprise.
