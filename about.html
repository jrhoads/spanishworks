About

Spanish Works provides interpreting and marketing in Spanish for businesses that want to communicate across cultures. Whether you are a small business owner wanting to increase your client base by marketing to the Hispanic community (30% in Providence according to the 2000 Census) or a lawyer needing to present a certified translation in court, Spanish Works is here for you. Let us help you improve your business by communicating across cultures with accuracy.
Why choose Spanish Works? You will receive:
  • Accurate and precise translations with correct shades of meaning and register
  • Certified and notarized translations
  • Specialized Education:
    • Master's Degree in Spanish Literature and Linguistics
    • Passed 2 national interpreting exams: NCSC’s legal interpreter test and CMI’s medical interpreting written test
    • 56 hours of interpreting and translation coursework
  • Experience working for law firms, respected medical clinics, and small businesses
